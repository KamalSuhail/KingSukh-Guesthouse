About Us


Welcome to our guest house, a place where comfort meets elegance. Nestled beside the serene Barshal Water Tank in Manpur, Barhanti, West Bengal, our guest house offers a tranquil retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Our rooms are designed with your comfort in mind, featuring modern amenities and tasteful decor. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or an extended stay, we strive to make your experience unforgettable.
